Three Generations

Pure Craft.Pure Heritage.

Al Andalus was founded in 1987 by Hajj Mustafa Badawi in the heart of Alexandria's old quarter. What began as a single attar cart near the El-Attarin mosque became the city's most trusted perfume house.

Today his grandsons blend ancient Andalusian recipes with rare materials sourced from India, Saudi Arabia, and Bulgaria — hand-blending methods unchanged for over thirty years.
