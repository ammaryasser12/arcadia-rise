Our Story

Born FromAlexandria's Edge.

Corniche House was founded by a family of Alexandria fishermen who believed the finest seafood deserved more than a plastic tray. We built our kitchen three meters from the sea — close enough to feel the salt in the air.

Every dish honors the Mediterranean heritage of our city. No frozen imports, no shortcuts. Just honest, beautiful cooking from a place that's been doing this for generations.
